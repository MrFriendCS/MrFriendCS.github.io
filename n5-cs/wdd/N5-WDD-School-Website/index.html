Castlebay Community School

Welcome

Castlebay Community School is an all-through school (3-18) on the Isle of Barra. It has 3 distinct departments:

  • Nursery
  • Primary
  • Secondary

Nursery

The Nursery is located in the Barra Children's Centre, across the road from the main school building.

Primary

The primary is housed in the old secondary school that was built in the 1930s.

Secondary

The secondary is housed in a purpose built building from the 1980s.

The Future

The school will be razed to the ground and from the ashes will rise a mighty hub. The hub will encompass the whole school, the hospital, and the care home, to name but a few of the functions that will flock to it.
